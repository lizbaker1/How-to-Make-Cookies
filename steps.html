Ingredients for Baking Cookies
  • 3/4 cups of granulated sugar

  • 3/4 cups of packed brown sugar

  • 1 cup of butter

  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla

  • 1 egg

  • 2 and 1/4 cup of flour

  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda

  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

The Steps

First, you should begin by gathering all ingredients. Then you should heat your oven to 375 degrees F. Mix sugar, butter, vanilla, and egg in a large bowl. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Then, stir in chocolate chips. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until light brown.
